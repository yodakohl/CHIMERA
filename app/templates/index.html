Automatic area scan

Request imagery for a latitude/longitude bounding box and the scanner will automatically download tiles from NASA's Global Imagery Browse Services (GIBS) before running the analysis pipeline on each tile.

Large areas may require increasing the tile size to stay under the built-in limit of 50 GIBS requests per scan.
